.who. we are

Netsalvo is a privately held company based in Singapore.

The founders of Netsalvo are experts in several fields with Silicon Valley experience. Our combined experience covers a broad-range of technologies such as mobile messaging, IP and telecoms networks, enterprise security solutions and embedded hardware systems.

.what. we do

Netsalvo performs research and development of innovative web and mobile applications and platforms for real-time communications. We also provide consulting services for mobile technologies and Rich Internet Application development, as well as solutions for “Web 2.0” enhancements for web communities.

We are currently developing applications that enable real-time chat for social networking and e-commerce websites.
